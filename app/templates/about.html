Hackjobs is Hackernews for Jobs

Hackjobs is a free open site to find jobs inspired by the simplicity of Hackernews.


What problem are we trying to solve?

Companies and startups post their job and internship openings in their sites.This is scattered throughout web.Sometimes people miss important openings. Hackjobs aims to become a community driven job listing site for programmers.


How hackjobs is different from other job listing sites?

Just like hackernews anyone can post details about job openings in hackjobs.Hackjobs do not connect with any companies or users in any manner. It is completely free to use for anyone.Only the title,link and short text details can be posted. The news expire based on time.


Is hackjobs related to hackernews or ycombinator?

No. Hackjobs is not related to hackernews or ycombinator in any manner. Hackjobs is inspired by the simplicity of hackernews. Hackjobs aims to become the online front page for jobs openings just like hackernews is for technews.


Is the site only for programming jobs?

Hackjobs only accepts jobs related to technology. It can be programming, hardware or anything that involves technical expertise.
